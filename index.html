Leadership & Board Advisory

At Terumo Corporation, our Leadership and Board Advisory team is dedicated to guiding the global advancement of medical technology. As a cornerstone of healthcare innovation, we ensure that every strategic decision is rooted in our mission of "Contributing to Society through Healthcare."

Industry Perspective

The global medical device industry is undergoing a profound transformation driven by precision engineering and digital integration. At Terumo Corporation, we view cross-border innovation not just as a business expansion, but as a commitment to enhancing patient outcomes through stable supply chains and regulatory excellence. Our perspective is built on a century of leadership in cardiovascular and hospital solutions, where we harmonize medical technology with the evolving demands of clinical environments. Sustainable value in this sector is created through the systematic alignment of high-quality product design, ethical distribution, and long-term institutional partnerships.

Professional Focus

At Terumo Corporation, our professional focus is dedicated to advancing the boundaries of medical intervention and patient safety. We specialize in four core pillars of clinical excellence: Cardiovascular Systems, including advanced bypass and monitoring technologies; Vascular Intervention products for minimally invasive therapy; Blood Management solutions that ensure a safe and stable supply; and General Hospital products that optimize daily care delivery. By integrating these specialized systems with advanced clinical data, we continue to develop comprehensive platforms that enhance surgical precision and diagnostic accuracy, ensuring high-quality care across our global healthcare networks.
